RECIPES

INGREDIENTS

1 cup frozen mixed berries

1/4 cup almond milk

5 tbsp natural Greek yogurt

1 tbsp crushed walnuts

Frozen pineapple, papaya and mango mix

1/2 cup orange juice

METHOD

Step 1 - strawberries smoothies

Add the chops banana in a blender with 1 cup of frozen berries, 1/4 cup almond milk, 5 tsp natural Greek yoghurt, crushed walnuts blend for 2-3 minutes until smooth.

Step 2

Pour the smoothie into a tall glass to serve.

Step 1 - mango smoothies

Add frozen pineapple, mango and papaya mix. Along with 1/2 cup orange juice blend for 2-3 minutes until smooth.

Step 2

Pour the smoothie into a tall glass to serve.
