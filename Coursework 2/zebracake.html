RECIPES

INGREDIENTS

1 1/2 cup all purpose flour

1 cup sugar

1 tbsp baking powder

1/4 tsp baking soda

A pinch of salt

1/2 cup vegetable oil

1tsp vinilla

2 eggs

1/2 cup water

2 tbsp cocoa powder

3 tbsp water

METHOD

Step 1

Add 2 eggs, blend until light and fluffy. Add 1 cup of caster sugar bit by bit. Keep blending. 1 tsp vanilla, 1/2 cup vegetable oil until the mixer look light and smooth.

Step 2

Now 1 1/2 cups of all purpose flour, 1/4 tsp baking soda, 1 tbsp baking powder and a pinch of salt on the sieve. Sift first half of dry ingredients until all is in the bach.

Step 3

Whisk together, add 1/2 a cup water little by little. Now sift second help of dry ingredients. Continue to work together until it's perfectly golden.

Step 4

In a bowl, add 2 tbsp of cocoa powder, 3 tbsp of water until a thick texture.

Step 5

Divide mixture into 2 separate bowls. Add cocoa paste to 1 bowl and mix well.

Step 6

Get a baking tray. Add the mixture one by one to create a pattern. Use a toothpick to make more patterns.

Step 7

Bake for 350'F/Gas mark 3&half for 35 minutes. Once it bakes leave it to cool down for 6 minutes.
