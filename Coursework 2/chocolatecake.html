RECIPES

INGREDIENTS

1 1/2 cups self raising flour

1 1/3 cups sugar

1/4 cup cocoa powder

1 1/2 baking powder

1/2 tsp baking soda

3 medium eggs

1/2 cup oil

1/3 cup milk

1/2 cup water with 1/2 tsp coffee

FOR GLAZE

3/4 cup water

1/3 cup sugar

3 tbsp cocoa powder

FOR ICING

1 cup double cream

1/4 cup icing sugar

1 tsp vanilla

METHOD

Step 1

Grease 2 x 20cm sandwich tins with a little butter and base line with baking paper. In a bowl, beat together the butter and sugar with an electric hand whisk until light and fluffy.

Step 2

Add the eggs, 1 at a time, beating after each addition. Sift in the flour, baking powder and cocoa, then lightly fold into the mixture.

Step 3

Add a little milk until you have a dropping consistency, then divide between the tins. Itâ€™s best to only fill the sandwich tins 3/4 full.

Step 4

Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean. Leave to cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 5

Melt the chocolate in a bowl over a pan of simmering water, then cool slightly. In a bowl, beat the butter and icing sugar together with an electric hand whisk until light and fluffy, then fold in the melted chocolate.

Step 6

Spread some icing over 1 of the cakes, top with the other cake and the rest of the icing.
